about.

ShuTing (she/her) here! Thank you for visiting my little internet nook. I'm an island born, city raised Chinese-Canadian currently based in Vancouver, BC. I'm pursuing my Computer Science degree at the University of British Columbia, progressing through my career journey as a software developer and doing the occasional dabble into design.

In my free time you can catch me on the badminton or volleyball courts, hiking local mountains, trying to become a polygot, making travel films and organizing hackathons at nwPlus! I'm extremely passionate about Diversity & Inclusion and love building events through cmd-f to support women*-identifying and underrepresented groups in their tech world explorations.

Let's chat! Or find my mini dragon bot in the corner who is eager to help break (melt) the ice. Try asking for my music reccomendations, what I'm currently reading or what language I want to learn next.
