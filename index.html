about.

ShuTing (she/her) here! Thank you for visiting my little internet nook. I'm an island born, city raised Chinese-Canadian currently based in Vancouver, BC. I'm pursuing my Computer Science degree at the University of British Columbia, progressing through my career journey as a software developer and occasionally dabbling in design.

In my free time you can catch me on the badminton or volleyball courts, hiking local mountains, making films, trying to become a polygot and organizing hackathons at nwPlus! I'm extremely passionate about Diversity & Inclusion and love building events to support women*-identifying and underrepresented groups in discovering their tech journeys through cmd-f.
